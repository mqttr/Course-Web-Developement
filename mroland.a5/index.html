About Me

My Name is Matthew Roland. I am a student at University of Nebraska at Omaha that is to say I am unemployed. I grew up in Papillion. It is a small town south-west of Omaha, Nebraska. There isn't much to see down there, but my favorite restaurant is near by. It is called Dragon Cafe. It is a chinese-american inspired restaurant.

I am excited to be going to University of Nebraska at Omaha. I have meet a lot of great friends, and joined a handful of clubs. In high school, I joined their Dungeons and Dragons club, but I am not sure if I will have the time to play Dungeons and Dragons in college. But the clubs that I did join are Nullify and Campus Fellowship. Nullify is a club that focuses on digital capture the flag games.

I am really interested in everything related to computers. I enjoy anything from building them to cryptography. My interest is peaked by video games and websites. 3d printers and IoT devices are cool.

Education

I have been to 4 schools in my life. I know it isn't a large number, but my family rarely moved. I went to Carriage Hill for Kindergarten. Saint Collumbkille for the rest of my elementary education. My high school diploma was given to me by Creighton Prepatory School. And now, I am at University of Nebraska at Omaha studying for a cyber security major.

Recreation

I have had a decent amount of hobbies while I was growing up. When I was really young, I used to play with Legos daily. But when I started getting older I had an imagination for random contraptions that I would find on youtube. I would rarely actually build any of these contraptions but I would still be constantly thinking about it. In high school, I did some more manly things including trap shooting for a little less than a year, and bow and arrow training, off and on for about 3 years.

Now I find myself busy with school work. But, when I'm not doing that some of the time I'm hanging out with friends. And when I'm not doing that, I usually play video games including Apex Legends, Fallout 4, and Valorant. I also own a 3d printer which I am constantly working with. And just the other day I started painting some of my 3d prints.
