Campus Fellowship

Campus Fellowship is a Christian group on campus. They are connected with a local church. They are full of people who take their relationship with God seriously.

Maker Group

The purpose of the UNO Maker Group is to support and grow STEM and MAKER activities at UNO and throughout the surrounding community. We do this by creating a supportive community of makers that can share knowledge and resources with each other. The club will have regularly scheduled sessions where members can bring their own projects to work on and collaborate with other makers in the community by sharing knowledge and equipment.

MavLabs

MavLabs is a community that empowers students to collaborate on creative projects to explore their interests and even develop new ones. Any student, no matter their skill level, is allowed to join an existing project or start a new project. If you don't want to join or start a project then that's fine as well, you can join us at our different club events to socialize and network with other students.
